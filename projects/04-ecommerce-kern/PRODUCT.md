# Product

## Register
brand

## Users
Urban man, 28–42. Buys fewer things, better things. Appreciates high craft, longevity, and considered design.

## Product Purpose
Premium menswear — shirts, trousers, outerwear. Limited runs. Ethically made. Built to last decades. The site serves as a high-craft, cinematic storytelling vessel to sell these garments, utilizing scroll-driven narratives to elevate the product.

## Brand Personality
Fear of God × Lemaire energy. Minimal words. Absolute confidence. The product speaks. The site gets out of its way.

## Anti-references
- No discounts
- No urgency timers
- No "LIMITED OFFER" banners
- No star rating widgets or standard customer reviews (use editorial validation instead)
- No generic SaaS layouts
- No bold or semibold font weights
- No clutter

## Design Principles
- Scroll-driven storytelling: the scroll itself becomes the narrative device (e.g., pinned sequential reveals).
- Unmissable primary action: only ONE filled button on the entire site (Add to Cart).
- Strictest font weight discipline: exclusively light and normal weights.
- Editorial validation over social proof.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Ensure high contrast for text against dark surfaces. Respect prefers-reduced-motion for all GSAP and Framer Motion animations.
