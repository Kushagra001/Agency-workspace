# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Local patients and families seeking high-quality, compassionate medical care. They are often stressed, looking for immediate reassurance, clear contact information, and easy appointment booking.

## Product Purpose
A local business landing experience for Medica Health Centre. Its primary goal is to establish trust, showcase the clinic's expertise, and drive conversions through a seamless Airtable-integrated booking form.

## Brand Personality
Trustworthy, compassionate, professional, and accessible. The tone should feel like a premium, established clinic that still offers warm, personal care.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS aesthetic (dark mode, purple/indigo gradients).
- Overly abstract or playful illustrations (health care requires seriousness).
- Chaotic or cluttered layouts that increase cognitive load for patients.

## Design Principles
- **Clinical but Warm:** Use clean whites and clinical blues, but soften them with organic shapes or photography.
- **Clear Conversion Paths:** The primary action (Booking) must always be obvious and frictionless.
- **Editorial Trust:** High-craft typography and intentional whitespace to signal premium quality.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast for readability, particularly for older patients or those with vision impairments.
- Clear, distinct focus states for form navigation.
- Reduced motion compliance for all GSAP animations.
