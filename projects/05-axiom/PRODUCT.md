# AXIOM STRATEGY — Product Brief

**Date:** 2026-05-11  
**Register:** Brand (funnel = product)  
**Status:** Phase 4/14 — Foundation complete, sections 5-14 pending

---

## Users & Intent

**Primary User:** Rohan Malhotra (the consultant — voice owner for all copy)

**Who visits the page:**
- Founders and CMOs at D2C brands or SaaS startups, ₹1–10Cr revenue
- Spending on ads but seeing flat returns
- Smart, skeptical, busy
- Will NOT fill a form; will book only if proven
- High intent but low patience

**Why they arrive:**
- Referral from satisfied past client or LinkedIn
- Searched "growth consultant" or "marketing systems"
- Saw a tweet or LinkedIn post about acquisition funnels

**Conversion goals (in priority order):**
1. Download the free "Growth Audit Framework" lead magnet (lowest friction)
2. Book a 30-minute strategy call directly (high intent path)

---

## Product Purpose

**This is a VSL-style one-page funnel,** not a website.

**One page. One goal: get the visitor to either download a lead magnet OR book a call.**

Everything else is in service of those two actions. No blog, no about page, no contact form. All decision-making supports these conversions.

## Brand Strategy

**Name:** Axiom Strategy  
**Founder/Consultant:** Rohan Malhotra  
**Tagline:** "Grow faster. With a system, not a guess."

**Positioning:**
High-ticket marketing consultant who helps D2C brands and SaaS startups build scalable customer acquisition systems. ₹2–5L per engagement. Books through strategy calls only — no inquiry forms.

**Tone & Voice:**
- **Direct.** Confident. Results-first. No fluff, no motivational quotes.
- Every word earns its place.
- Data-driven. Specific metrics, not vague promises.
- "Here's what broken, here's what I'd fix, here's the proof."
- No "discover," no "innovative," no "synergize."

**Anti-references:**
- ❌ SaaS agency playbook (over-designed, generic CTAs)
- ❌ Life coach energy (emotional, vague)
- ❌ Crypto bro confidence (loud, all-caps, memes)
- ❌ Consulting firm stuffiness (impersonal, jargon)

**What it should feel like:**
- Ali Abdaal's site (direct, personal, premium but warm)
- High-end consulting firms (competence-first, few words)
- Dickie Bird's newsletter landing pages (editorial, credible)

## Content Pillars (Page Structure)

1. **Hero** — VSL-style opener: "Your ads are running. Your growth isn't."
2. **Proof Ticker** — scrolling results (dark strip, constant motion = constant proof)
3. **Problem** — agitate the pain (checklist: the problems they face)
4. **Solution** — the Axiom method (3 pillars: Diagnose, Build, Scale)
5. **Results** — case study metrics (3 clients, real numbers, countUp animation)
6. **Process** — how an engagement works (4 weeks, week-by-week, transparent)
7. **Testimonials** — founder quotes + results (social proof, emotional anchor)
8. **Lead Magnet** — free framework download (2-column: offer left, form right)
9. **About Rohan** — credibility + photo (image + credentials, not corporate bio)
10. **Book Call** — Calendly embed (final ask, "free 30-minute call")
11. **Footer** — minimal (privacy, unsubscribe, LinkedIn link)
12. **Thank You** — post-lead-magnet page (confirm delivery, upsell to call)

## Core Messaging

### Hero Promise
"Your ads are running. Your growth isn't."  
"Most D2C brands and SaaS startups spend ₹5–50L on marketing and can't tell you which ₹1 is working. I fix that. In 90 days, with a system you own."

### Problem Agitation (Pain Points)
- Can't tie ad spend to revenue
- Agencies send beautiful reports that don't explain flat sales
- Content team hired but organic growth is unpredictable
- Every consultant pitches something different; no framework
- Best month was a fluke; can't repeat it

### Solution Frame
Three steps: **Diagnose** (audit + findings) → **Build** (live system) → **Scale** (hand you the playbook)

### Social Proof
- 47 founders joined this month
- ₹2.4Cr in Q3 revenue attributed
- 340% ROAS improvement (skincare D2C)
- 3.2× conversion rate lift
- 8% → 2.1% churn reduction

### Call to Action Hierarchy
1. "Get the free growth audit framework" — low-friction entry
2. "Book a 30-minute strategy call" — high-intent path
3. "No pitch. No deck. We'll look at your numbers. Free." — trust signal

## Anti-Patterns to Reject

- ❌ Form fields asking for company size / budget before the ask (friction too early)
- ❌ Infinite scroll or pinned sections (funnel readers scroll fast)
- ❌ Testimonial videos (no time, slow to load)
- ❌ "Learn more" nested buttons (one click to one conversion)
- ❌ Stock photos of smiling consultants (credibility = Rohan's photo only)
- ❌ "Limited spots available" false scarcity (undermines trust)

---

## Conversion Tracking

- **Lead Magnet Download:** Email capture, Resend welcome email, add to ConvertKit nurture
- **Call Booking:** Calendly tracking (already built-in)
- **Post-Download:** 5-email sequence (Axiom's nurture automation case study)

---

## Technical Constraints

- Single-page funnel: no sub-routes except `/thank-you`
- Mobile-first: must convert at 375px
- Animations increase trust or reduce friction only (no distractions)
- No third-party embeds that slow page load (Calendly script deferred)
- Lighthouse score target: ≥90 across all metrics

---

## Success Metrics

1. **Hero section engagement:** 40%+ scroll below fold
2. **Lead magnet downloads:** 8–12% of visitors
3. **Call bookings:** 2–4% of visitors (from both hero CTA and post-download)
4. **Page speed:** Lighthouse ≥90
5. **Mobile conversion rate:** ≥1.5× of desktop (easier on mobile)
